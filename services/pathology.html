Common questions

Pathology: your questions answered

What tests does your pathology lab offer?

Our in-house laboratory offers routine and specialised blood tests, health check-up packages, histopathology and other investigations, supported by colour-Doppler, sonography, X-ray, IVP and ECG, all under one roof.

How quickly are reports ready?

Because the laboratory is in-house and open alongside the 24x7 hospital, most routine reports are available the same day, with timely reporting for urgent and admitted patients.

Who leads pathology at the hospital?

Dr Vinita Jain, a consultant pathologist with MBBS and MD (Pathology), leads the diagnostic laboratory, supporting accurate diagnosis across every department.
