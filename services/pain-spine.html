Common questions

Pain Management & Spine: your questions answered

Can slip disc and sciatica be treated without surgery?

In most cases, yes. We offer non-surgical and minimally invasive treatments such as nerve blocks, ozone discectomy and endoscopic procedures that relieve pain and help you avoid open surgery.

What is ozone discectomy?

Ozone discectomy is a minimally invasive day-care procedure that shrinks a bulging disc using an ozone-oxygen mixture, relieving pressure on the nerve with little downtime.

Who treats spine and pain at the hospital?

Dr Naveen Soni, an interventional pain physician with an MD and a Fellowship in Pain Medicine from Germany, leads our spine and pain care.
