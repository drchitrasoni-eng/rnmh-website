Common questions

General Medicine: your questions answered

What does a general physician treat?

A general physician diagnoses and manages common and chronic adult conditions, including diabetes, thyroid disorders, high blood pressure, respiratory and gastric problems, migraine, fevers and seasonal illness, and coordinates care with other specialists when needed.

Can I get help managing diabetes and thyroid here?

Yes. We provide ongoing management of diabetes, thyroid and blood pressure with personalised treatment plans and in-house laboratory testing for regular monitoring.

Who leads general medicine at the hospital?

Dr Vishnu Gupta, a consultant physician with MBBS and MD (General Medicine) and over a decade of experience, leads general medicine care.
