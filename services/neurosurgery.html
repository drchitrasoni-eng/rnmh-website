Common questions

Neurosurgery: your questions answered

Is all spine surgery done through a big cut?

No. Many spine conditions are treated with minimally invasive, motion-preserving techniques, and surgery is recommended only when conservative care has not helped.

What brain conditions do you treat?

We treat brain tumours, head injuries and other neurosurgical conditions, with both surgical and non-surgical options discussed for each patient.

Who is the neurosurgeon?

Dr Pavan Jain, a neurosurgeon with an M.Ch in Neurosurgery from GMC Goa and 16+ years of experience, provides brain and spine care.
