Common questions

Neurosurgery: patient FAQs

When should I consult a neurosurgeon?

Consult a neurosurgeon for severe neurological symptoms, brain or spine injury, progressive weakness, spinal tumour concerns or conditions requiring surgical opinion.

Is all spine surgery open surgery?

No. Some spine conditions may be managed with minimally invasive or motion-preserving techniques, while others may not need surgery.
