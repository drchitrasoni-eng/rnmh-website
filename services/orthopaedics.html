Common questions

Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement: your questions answered

How long is recovery after knee replacement?

Most patients begin walking with support within a day or two and steadily improve over a few weeks, guided by a structured physiotherapy plan.

What is arthroscopy?

Arthroscopy is keyhole joint surgery using a small camera, used to diagnose and treat knee, shoulder and other joint problems with less pain and faster recovery.

Who leads orthopaedics at the hospital?

Dr Chirag Jain, an orthopaedic, joint replacement and trauma surgeon with an MS in Orthopaedics, leads our orthopaedic care.
