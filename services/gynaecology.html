Common questions

Gynaecology & Fertility: your questions answered

Do you offer painless (epidural) delivery?

Yes. We offer normal painless delivery with epidural analgesia as well as caesarean section, with round-the-clock obstetric and newborn care including an NICU.

Can fibroids and ovarian cysts be removed by keyhole surgery?

Yes. Many gynaecological conditions, including fibroids and ovarian cysts, can be treated with laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, which means smaller cuts and a faster recovery.

Who leads gynaecology and fertility care?

Dr Chitra Soni, an obstetrician, gynaecologist and infertility specialist with an MS in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, leads women's health at the hospital.
