Common questions

Urology: your questions answered

Can kidney stones be removed without open surgery?

Yes. Most kidney and ureteric stones are treated with laser and keyhole techniques such as URS and PCNL, which avoid large cuts and speed up recovery.

How is an enlarged prostate treated?

An enlarged prostate is commonly treated with TURP, a minimally invasive procedure that relieves urinary symptoms without external cuts.
