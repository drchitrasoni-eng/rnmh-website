Common questions

General & Laparoscopic Surgery: your questions answered

What is laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery?

Laparoscopic surgery uses small cuts and a camera instead of one large incision. It usually means less pain, lower infection risk and a quicker return to normal life.

Is laser treatment for piles painful?

Laser piles treatment is a minimally invasive day-care procedure with minimal pain and bleeding and a fast recovery, compared with traditional open surgery.

Who performs general and laparoscopic surgery?

Dr Robin Bothra, a general and laparoscopic surgeon with MBBS, MS and FMAS and over twenty years of experience, leads our surgery department.
