Common questions

ENT: your questions answered

What ENT problems do you treat?

We treat the full range of ear, nose and throat conditions for adults and children, including ear infections, hearing loss, sinusitis, nasal blockage, tonsil and adenoid problems, vertigo and snoring, along with ENT surgery where needed.

Do you perform ENT surgery for children?

Yes. Common paediatric procedures such as tonsil and adenoid surgery and grommet insertion for ear infections are performed with care for younger patients.

Who leads ENT care at the hospital?

Dr N S Rathore, an ENT surgeon with MBBS and MS (ENT) and over a decade of experience, leads ENT care. He consults in English, Hindi and Jaipuri.
