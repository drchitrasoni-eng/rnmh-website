Accreditations & Government Schemes

We are committed to providing high-quality healthcare that is accessible to everyone. R N Multispeciality Hospital is fully accredited and empanelled with major government health schemes and private insurers.

Our Accreditations

NABH Accredited

We meet the stringent national quality standards set by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers, ensuring patient safety and high-quality care at every step.

ABDM Compliant

We are fully compliant with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, allowing for secure, seamless digital health records and better continuity of care.

Schemes & Cashless Insurance

We accept a wide range of government schemes and private insurance for cashless treatment:

  • Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)
  • RGHS (Rajasthan Government Health Scheme)
  • MAA Yojana (Mukhyamantri Ayushman Aarogya Yojana)
  • Chiranjeevi Yojana
  • All major TPAs and Cashless Health Insurance
