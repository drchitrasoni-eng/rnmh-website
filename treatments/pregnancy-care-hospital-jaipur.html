Pregnancy Care Hospital in Jaipur

Pregnancy care and delivery support in Jaipur at RNMH, near Mansarovar Metro Station. R N Multispeciality Hospital is a 50-bed multispeciality hospital near Mansarovar Metro Station with diagnostics, emergency support and cashless insurance assistance under one roof.

When should you consult a doctor?

  • Missed period or confirmed pregnancy
  • Regular antenatal check-ups
  • High-risk pregnancy factors
  • Reduced baby movement or bleeding
  • Labour pain or fluid leakage

Diagnosis

  • Antenatal check-ups
  • Ultrasound and blood tests
  • Blood pressure, sugar and fetal monitoring

Treatment options

Treatment depends on doctor evaluation. Suitability varies by condition, severity, age, medical history and investigation findings.

  • Routine antenatal care
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
  • Normal, painless or caesarean delivery planning
  • NICU support when needed

Insurance support

RNMH supports cashless insurance, TPA, RGHS, PM-JAY and MAA Yojana where applicable. Approval depends on policy terms, scheme rules and TPA or government approval.
