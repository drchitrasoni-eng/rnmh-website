When should you consult a doctor?

  • Bleeding during stool
  • Pain or swelling near the anus
  • Itching or discomfort
  • Recurrent constipation with pain
  • Symptoms that do not improve with medication

Diagnosis

  • Clinical examination by a surgeon
  • Proctoscopy or related evaluation where needed
  • Assessment of grade and severity

Treatment options

Treatment depends on doctor evaluation. Suitability varies by condition, severity, age, medical history and investigation findings.

  • Medicines and lifestyle changes
  • Minimally invasive procedures
  • Laser treatment where suitable
  • Surgery when clinically indicated

Insurance support

RNMH supports cashless insurance, TPA, RGHS, PM-JAY and MAA Yojana where applicable. Approval depends on policy terms, scheme rules and TPA or government approval.
