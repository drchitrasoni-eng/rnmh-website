Kidney Stone Treatment in Jaipur

When should you consult a doctor?

  • Severe pain in side or back
  • Burning urination or blood in urine
  • Nausea, vomiting or fever
  • Repeated urinary infection

Diagnosis

  • Urine tests and blood tests
  • Ultrasound or CT KUB
  • Stone size and location assessment

Treatment options

Treatment depends on doctor evaluation. Suitability varies by condition, severity, age, medical history and investigation findings.

  • Medicines and hydration advice for small stones
  • URS, PCNL or laser procedures where suitable
  • Emergency care if infection or obstruction is present

Insurance support

RNMH supports cashless insurance, TPA, RGHS, PM-JAY and MAA Yojana where applicable. Approval depends on policy terms, scheme rules and TPA or government approval.
