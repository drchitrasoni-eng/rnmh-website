When should you consult a doctor?

  • Severe knee arthritis
  • Pain despite medicines and physiotherapy
  • Difficulty walking or climbing stairs
  • Bent or deformed knee

Diagnosis

  • X-ray and clinical assessment
  • Medical fitness evaluation
  • Discussion of benefits, risks and recovery

Treatment options

Treatment depends on doctor evaluation. Suitability varies by condition, severity, age, medical history and investigation findings.

  • Non-surgical care for early arthritis
  • Partial or total knee replacement where indicated
  • Physiotherapy-led recovery plan

Insurance support

RNMH supports cashless insurance, TPA, RGHS, PM-JAY and MAA Yojana where applicable. Approval depends on policy terms, scheme rules and TPA or government approval.
