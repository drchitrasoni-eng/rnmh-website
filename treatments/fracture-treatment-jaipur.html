Fracture Treatment in Jaipur

Orthopaedic consultation for bone, joint and fracture care at RNMH, Main Kings Road, Nirman Nagar.

Book on WhatsApp Call Now: 0141 239 0320

When should you consult a doctor?

  • Pain, swelling or deformity after injury
  • Inability to move or bear weight
  • Open wound with suspected fracture
  • Numbness or severe pain

Diagnosis

  • X-ray and clinical examination
  • CT scan where needed
  • Assessment of blood supply and nerve function

Treatment options

Treatment depends on doctor evaluation. Suitability varies by condition, severity, age, medical history and investigation findings.

  • Splint, plaster or brace
  • Closed reduction where suitable
  • Surgery and fixation when required

Insurance support

RNMH supports cashless insurance, TPA, RGHS, PM-JAY and MAA Yojana where applicable. Approval depends on policy terms, scheme rules and TPA or government approval.
