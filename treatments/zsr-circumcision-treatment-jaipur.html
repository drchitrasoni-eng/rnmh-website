When should you consult a surgeon?

  • Tight foreskin or phimosis
  • Repeated balanitis or foreskin infection
  • Pain, cracking or difficulty while retracting the foreskin
  • Doctor-advised circumcision for medical reasons
  • Questions about ZSR or stapler circumcision suitability

The first step is a private consultation and examination. Dr Robin Bothra explains whether ZSR circumcision, conventional circumcision or another plan is appropriate for the patient.

  • Pre-surgery tests when clinically needed

Treatment approach

ZSR circumcision is a stapler-based circumcision technique. Suitability, recovery and procedure choice depend on doctor evaluation, age, anatomy, infection status and medical history.

  • Consultation-first decision on ZSR versus other options
  • Procedure performed in a hospital setting
  • Clear post-procedure wound care and follow-up instructions
  • 24x7 hospital support for urgent concerns
